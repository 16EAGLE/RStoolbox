Description

Calculateds angle and magnitude of change vectors. Dimensionality is limited to two bands.

Usage

rasterCVA(x, y, ...)

Arguments

x

RasterBrick or RasterStack with two layers.

y

RasterBrick or RasterStack with two layers.

...

further arguments passed to writeRaster
